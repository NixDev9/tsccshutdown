Dear TSCC (trackSanta.cc) Community,

Announcement: Shutdown of TSCC

We regret to inform you that TSCC (trackSanta.cc) will be shutting down. Despite our best efforts, we have decided to discontinue our operations due to time constraints and the presence of numerous other Santa tracking services.

We started our journey in 2022, We wanted people to have the best Santa Tracking Experience But August 13 2023 TSCC shut down, TSCC will come back one day.

We sincerely thank you for your support and enthusiasm throughout our time together. It has been a joy to be part of your holiday traditions.

For those seeking Santa tracking services, we encourage you to explore the many other reputable Santa trackers available.

Thank you for being a part of the TSCC community.


Warm regards,
The TSCC (trackSanta.cc) Team
